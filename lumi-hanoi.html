Tenant Profile and Rental Demand

The tenant profile at Lumi Hanoi is expected to include professionals working in nearby business parks, employees of technology and manufacturing companies in the western corridor, and international families who prefer newer buildings with modern facilities.

As with all Hanoi projects, actual rental demand depends on unit layout, furnishing quality, floor, tower position, and market timing. Owners should not assume strong tenant demand without a proper rental readiness review of the specific unit.

Foreign Ownership and Quota

Lumi Hanoi has been included in Hanoi's foreign ownership approval list, with approximately 1,181 apartments across nine towers approved for sale to foreign buyers. This represents nearly 30% of the project's total units.

Important: Foreign ownership quota can change. Buyers should verify quota availability for their specific tower and unit type before paying a deposit.
