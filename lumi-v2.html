Why Foreign Owners Choose Lumi Hanoi

For many foreign investors from Hong Kong, Singapore, Taiwan, and Korea, the CapitaLand name provides a level of confidence that is not always available with local developers. CapitaLand's track record in Vietnam and across Asia means buyers have a reference point for expected build quality, handover standards, and project governance.

The western Hanoi location, while less established than central districts, is positioned for long-term infrastructure growth. Metro Lines 5, 6, and 7 are planned to improve connectivity to the city centre.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can foreigners buy apartments at Lumi Hanoi?

Lumi Hanoi has been approved for foreign ownership subject to the 30% quota per building. Buyers should verify current quota availability before deposit.

What is the typical rental yield at Lumi Hanoi?

Rental yields in western Hanoi typically range from 2-3% gross annually. Actual yield depends on unit type, furnishing quality, and market timing.

How does Cozyhome charge for property management?

Cozyhome charges a percentage of monthly rent collected, typically 10-15% depending on service scope. Contact us for a detailed quote.

Can Cozyhome help with handover inspection?

Yes. Cozyhome provides professional handover inspection services to identify defects before formal acceptance.

How long does it take to find a tenant?

Tenant sourcing typically takes 2-4 weeks for well-furnished units in good condition, depending on market demand and pricing.
